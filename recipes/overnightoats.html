This is the way I make my base for Overnight Oats! This allows me to add any other toppings in order to create diversity in what I eat, and is tasty on its own.

Ingredients

  • Oats - 40g
  • Choice of protein powder - One scoop
  • Chia seeds - 2 tablespoons
  • Dairy-free greek yogurt - 200g

Steps

  1. Pour all dry ingredients (oats, chia seeds, and protein) into mason jar. I personally like to layer them to make it look more aesthetically pleasing.
  2. Add greek yogurt.
  3. Mix all ingredients together.
  4. In between these steps you can add your favourite fruits, or any other ingredients you want to make your overnight oats taste the way you want them too.
