Protein Pancakes

The ONLY protein pancake recipe you will ever need!

This pancake recipe will be one of the easiest pancakes you will ever make! They only require 4 ingredients. You read that correctly, ONLY 5 ingredients. You can add any toppings you want to this recipe to make it your own. GET CREATIVE!

Ingredients

  • eggs - 2
  • Oatmeal - 40 grams
  • 1 scoop of favourite protein powder
  • Ripe Banana - 1
  • Choice of cooking oil (butter, cooking spray, or oil)

Steps

  1. add eggs, oatmeal, protein to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, then add banana, and blend till the mixture becomes like a smoothie consistency.
  3. Heat oil of choice on a frying pan at low heat.
  4. Pour mixture on to heated pan to desired size.
  5. Once small bubbles form on the top, check bottom, if it has reached a golden brown, flip it and cook till finished.
