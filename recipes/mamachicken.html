Mama Sitas Chicken Breast

This recipe is a staple in my diet to help me lose weight, as it is tasty and the macros are super friendly! Lets making dieting easy.

Ingredients

  • Mama Sitas Marinade - 1/2cup
  • Chicken Breast - 1 kg
  • Ground Pepper - 1 tsp
  • Salt - Pinch of salt
  • Sesame oil - 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breast into bite size pieces or desired size.
  2. Grab a bowl, and add the chikcen breast.
  3. On Top of the chicken breast add your dry ingredients (salt and pepper) and give it a good mix with your hands.
  4. Add Mama Sitas BBQ marinade and mix with your hands.
  5. Let chicken breast marinade for at least 30 minutes. For best results overnight!
  6. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees fehrenheit
  7. Add chicken to air fryer and allow to cook for 15 minutes, or to an internal temperature of 165 degrees fehrenheit.
  8. Once cooked enjoy with your carb or vegetables of choice (I usually like to fry up some garlic rice).
